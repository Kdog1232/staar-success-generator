How to Use This Tool (Important)

This tool is designed for differentiated instruction, meaning the rigor changes based on the level you select.

  • Below Level → Designed for intervention, small group, and Tier 2/3 support
  • On Level → Aligned to grade-level STAAR expectations (this is your standard classroom rigor)
  • Advanced → Higher-level thinking, deeper reasoning, and challenge questions

💡 Best Practice: Most teachers generate 2–3 versions:
• On Level (whole group)
• Below Level (support)
• Advanced (challenge)

If you're checking rigor, make sure you're using On Level or Advanced.
